Ramayana is one of the two major Sanskrit epics of ancient India, the other being the Mahabharata.Along with the Mahabharata, it forms the Hindu Itihasa.The epic, traditionally ascribed to the Maharishi Valmiki, narrates the life of <Rama, the legendary Kosala Kingdom. It follows his fourteen-year exile to the forest by his father King Dasharatha, on request of his step-mother Kaikeyi, his travels across forests in India with his wife Sita and brother Lakshmana, the kidnapping of his wife by Ravana, the great king of Lanka, resulting in a war with him and Ram's eventual return to Ayodhya to be crowned king.This is the culmination point of the epic.
